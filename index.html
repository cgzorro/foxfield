Who’s behind it

An operator who builds software — not the other way around.

Foxfield is designed and built by Chris Fox, drawing on years on retail nursery floors here in Kansas City. Every workflow in the suite comes from real friction — the customer question nobody on staff could answer, the farm sheet that didn’t match the vendor packet, the plant data that lived in three places and agreed in none. This isn’t software guessing at the nursery business; it’s the nursery business, written down.
