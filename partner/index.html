The cost of the gap · one real Saturday

Epicor says 64.
The bench says 8.

Nepeta 'Cat's Pajamas' — a top-seller that flies off the bench in spring. The farm sheet promised 24. The system swears there are 64. There are 8, at $14.99 each. A customer drove across town to buy 20, took the 8 that were actually there, and left disappointed enough not to come back.

64
what Epicor claims is on hand
8
what's actually on the bench
24
what the farm sheet promised

Epicor is the system of record; it just can't track the floor in real time between receiving, the bench, and the customer. That gap falls on the staff who take the blame, the customer who found eight, and the business that quietly loses a regular — and it's the exact gap Orderflow closes the moment the truck doesn't match the sheet — one motion at the dock: scan the cart's QR, confirm what's right, tap what came up short.
